Tribute to Dr Abdul Qadeer Khan

About Dr Abdul Qadeer Khan

Abdul Qadeer Khan (1936–2021) was a Pakistani nuclear physicist and metallurgical engineer widely revered as the "father of Pakistan's nuclear weapons program". He established the Khan Research Laboratories (KRL) in 1976 and played a critical role in developing uranium enrichment centrifuges, enabling Pakistan to become the first Muslim nuclear power in 1998.
